About This Digest

This digest serves as a monthly update on the current EEG foundation model literature on arXiv. We filter with arXiv title and abstract keywords, and a triage LLM to decide on papers that qualify. Then, we generate a summary of the entire paper with an LLM. I manually choose the featured paper of the month.
